Meat Loaf


Desciption

This easy meatloaf recipe doesn't take long to make at all, and it's very good!



Ingredients

  • - 1 Â½ pounds ground beef

  • - 1 egg

  • - 1 onion chopped

  • - 1 cup milk

  • - salt and pepper

  • - 1/3rd cup ketchup

  • - 2 tablespoons brown sugar

  • - 2 tablespoons prepared mustard

Step by step directions

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. 2. Combine ground beef, onion, milk, bread crumbs and egg in a large bowl; season with salt and pepper. Transfer into prepared loaf pan.

  3. 3. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together in a small bowl until well combined; pour over meatloaf and spread it evenly over the top.

  4. 4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
